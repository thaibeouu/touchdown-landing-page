About Touchdown

You're in a long-distance relationship. You send and receive tons of messages everyday, not to mention hours of videochat from time to time. You can't get enough of each other and want to touch your counterpart. What now?

Enters Touchdown.

Touchdown is a device sophisticatedly designed to simulate the act of human touch, and by doing so introduces a whole different realm of values to your long-distance conversations.
